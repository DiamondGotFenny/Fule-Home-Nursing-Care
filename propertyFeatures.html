Elderly Care Center

The center covers an area of 2,000 square meters, with 5 floors above ground and 1 underground parking lot with a total gross floor area of 7,000 square meters. There are 250 sets of living units, 320 beds, roof gardens in it.

  • 4 types of units, including: large suite(with bathroom and living room), large single room(with bathroom), small single room and couple unit(with bathroom) to meet different living needs. In addition, family escort rooms are offered.
  • Each bedroom is equipped with electric nursing bed, a television, integral fixed furniture, sliding door, intelligent air purification system, Heating and cooling air conditioner and deodorizing equipment, as well as an intelligent elderly health electronic monitoring system and emergency call bell.
  • The Shower: The latest bath system offering hand-held and side sprayer bars with mist, multi-functional setting.
  • The building contains conference public hall for each floor, offices, staff quarters, restaurant, kitchen, boiler room, laundry and garage.
  • Lift,mobility aids, crutches and wheelchairs
