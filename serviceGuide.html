Professional Medical Care and Support

We offer individualised medical and nursing care to our senior residents as well as rehabilitation programmes and social engagement activities. Our services consist of:

  • 24-hour professional nursing care and instant care support.
  • Medical and rehabilitation clinic.
  • Periodic medical assessment.
  • A visiting doctor.
  • A routine health check-up
  • Special care in the Medical Center: Nasal tube care, catheter care, cognitive disorders, etc.
  • Cerebrospinal Rehabilitation Treatment Program: Stroke/Cognitive Disorder/Berkinson.
  • Elderly mental health counseling and treatment.
  • Individual care plan designed specifically to suit each resident’s needs.

*The medical and rehabilitation service in the contract will be offered to the residents in the care center at no charge. Other services that out of contract are charged separately, but the residents in Fule Home can enjoy a certain discount. In addition to serving residents of the Care Center, the Medical Center will also be open to the public, providing medical examination services, rehabilitation services and treatment services for various age-related diseases.


Care for the Elderly

The Elderly Care Center adopts membership system: members pay the basic membership fee for the residential care service , and need to pay a deposit and nursing fee as well. If a member pays a membership fee of 5-10 years at a time, a certain discount can be obtained. Our membership service including:

  • A cosy and home-like living environment for resident.
  • Assistance with activities of daily living and personal hygiene of the residents by the care workers,such as feeding, bathing, hair washing and changing clothes etc.
  • Catering: A design a menu adviced by registered dietitian's professional. Provision of three meals a day and afternoon tea.
  • Administration and supervision of medication;
  • Free to use the Recreation and Welfare Center. Therapeutic exercise and treatment

To be eligible for admission to Fule Home Nursing Care Center for the elderly, an applicant should be:

  • Aged 60 or above;
  • Assessed to be suitable for admission to the care center through the our care managers.
  • Obtain consent from the applicant and immediate family member, spouse
  • Mentally suitable for communal living

Services Open to Our Community:

  • Day-care only Service
  • Temporary care, including: Emergency Placement Service and Residential Respite Service
  • Specialised medical service to elderly

*A Reference to the Charge of Service

  • Small single room: monthly basic fee of 2,300 yuan; large single room: monthly basic fee of 2,800 yuan; couple room: monthly basic fee of 3,300 yuan; suite: monthly basic fee of 4,500 yuan.
  • Daycare costs 50 yuan to 100 yuan per day.
  • Temporary care charges 100 yuan to 300 yuan.
  • A depositneeded to be paid before the elderly entering the care center; the ranging from 10,000 to 50,000 yuan, depending on the room standard and the physical condition of the elderly.
  • Accident and medical risk insurance (the charge rate depending on the physical condition of the elderly)
  • Medical examination fee: 100 to 500 yuan(the charge rate depending on the physical condition of the elderly)
  • Nursing care fee: depending on the physical and mental condition of the elderly, 400 yuan/month for Low-care-level, 800 yuan/month for Medium-care-level, 1,200 yuan/month for High-care-level.
  • Medical rehabilitation costs are calculated on a case-by-case basis.
  • For home support and care cost, please inquiry our care manager

Recreation and Counselling

The Recreation and Welfare Center has a large number of recreational facilities, as well as 6,000 square meters of front and rear gardens, which can satisfy the residents' hobbies and interests, and is conducive to the healthy development of the elderly.

  • Access to all facilities in The Recreation and Welfare Center.
  • Cultural and recreational activities will be organizedon a regular basis, such as festival celebrations, birthday parties, sports exercises or newspapers, etc.
  • Volunteers will be arranged to visit for improving the mental and mental health of the elderly.
  • Social relationships development
  • Badminton and dancing area in the garden

*The Recreation and Welfare Center is not open to the public and only serves members.
