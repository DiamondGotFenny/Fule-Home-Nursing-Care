JOIN FULE HOME

Our aim is to provide dignified and temperature-based residential care services for the elderly and their families. We are actively providing excellent care and healthy services to the elderly in need. We sincerely invite people who are interested in optimizing the quality of life of the elderly to join us. Our team enhances the professional image and recognition of the industry and contributes to the smart ageing city.

We also focus on employee benefits, providing competitive compensation, year-end bonus, work-hard bonus, medical service, friendly working environment, professional on-the-job training, and good career prospect. We are committed to making our employees' lives as good as our residents.
